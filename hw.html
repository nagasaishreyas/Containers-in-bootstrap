Emotions in art

Emotions in art are the feelings that a piece of art evokes in the viewer. Art can evoke a wide range of emotions, including pleasure, interest, confusion, surprise, and more.

Hard Work in Art

Whenever you have two colours, or more importantly two values adjacent to one another, a boundary is formed between them. If the border between these two is exact with no blending or overlap - this is called a “hard edge”.
